3. DESCRIPTION COMPLÈTE (DESCRIPTION)
==========================================

Points Master - The Ultimate Strategy Game

Master the classic game of Dots and Squares in this modern, competitive mobile experience. Challenge yourself, your friends, or players worldwide in this strategic puzzle game that's easy to learn but hard to master.

HOW TO PLAY
Connect two adjacent points to create a segment. Complete the four sides of a square to score a point. The player with the most points at the end wins. Strategy is key - plan your moves carefully to block opponents and maximize your scoring opportunities.

GAME MODES

SOLO MODE
Practice against artificial intelligence with adjustable difficulty levels. Perfect for training and improving your skills offline.

DUO MODE
Play with a friend on the same device. Take turns on one screen - ideal for face-to-face competition.

QUICK MATCH
Find an opponent online instantly and compete in real-time matches. Every victory counts towards your global ranking.

PRIVATE GAMES
Create a private room and invite friends via QR code or direct invitation. Challenge specific players and play at your own pace.

KEY FEATURES

REAL-TIME MULTIPLAYER
Experience seamless online gameplay with instant synchronization. Play with players around the world in competitive ranked matches.

GLOBAL LEADERBOARD
Climb the ranks and compete for the top position. Track your progress and see how you compare to the best players.

DETAILED STATISTICS
Monitor your performance with comprehensive statistics. Track your win rate, best scores, completed squares, and winning streaks.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
Never miss a turn. Receive instant notifications when it's your move, when you receive invitations, or when a game ends.

CUSTOMIZABLE EXPERIENCE
Personalize your gameplay with adjustable music and sound effects. Control audio settings to match your preferences.

SMOOTH GAMEPLAY
Enjoy fluid animations and responsive controls designed for an optimal mobile gaming experience.

SOCIAL FEATURES
Build your gaming profile, customize your avatar, and challenge friends. Stay connected with your gaming community.

Perfect for strategy game enthusiasts, competitive players, and anyone who loves puzzle games. Download Points Master now and prove you're the ultimate master of strategy!
